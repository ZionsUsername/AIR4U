What it means to breathe

Speaking from experience, life can be very overwhelming, and sometimes it feels like what's overwhelming you is bigger than you can imagine. AIR4U wants to encourage people to take a minute to look away from the screen, step outside, and breathe. Personally, I have found this practice to be very revitalizing, and I hope you feel the same.
